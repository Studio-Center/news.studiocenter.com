“Studio Center Named One Of The
BEST PLACES TO WORK IN VIRGINIA
For The Second Consecutive Year”

Virginia Beach, VA - Studio Center, headquartered in Virginia Beach, Va., has recently been named as one of the 2014 Best Places to Work in Virginia. Virginia Business and Best Companies Group created the annual list of “Best Places to Work”.

This survey and award program was designed to identify, recognize and honor the best places of employment in Virginia, benefiting the state's economy, its work force and businesses. Studio Center has been named one of the Best Places to Work in Virginia in the small business category.

“We are all truly honored and humbled to be on this exclusive list. We thank Virginia Business and Best Companies Group for continually motivating all of us here at Studio Center to be the absolute best place that we can be EVERY year,” said William “Woody” Prettyman, Studio Center CEO.
