Studio Center Wins 19 Prestigious Telly Awards

Virginia Beach, VA - May 20, 2015.  Studio Center, headquartered in Virginia Beach, Va., has recently been awarded 19 national 2015 Telly Awards.  Each year, The Telly Awards honor and showcase the highest standards of excellence in Television Commercials, Video, Film, and Digital.

Winners of the 36th Annual Telly Awards were selected from over 12,000 entries from all 50 states and 5 continents.

Studio Center was honored for projects produced for organizations and businesses including Barbasol, Slover Library, Chrysler Museum, Ryka, ATAC, A1 American, Goodwill, Pure Silk, Goodman and Sons, WyoLotto, Circle of Sisterhood, Laser Health Spa, City of Portsmouth, G2 Ops, Genworth and Massimo Zenetti. The company also won an award for a Studio Center Branding Video called “Coach Speak”.

William “Woody” Prettyman, CEO of Studio Center, commented, “All of us really push each year to improve in all areas of our creative. Winning 19 Telly Awards validates everyone’s passion, commitment and excellence. This is our best showing ever so we’re both honored and thrilled!”
