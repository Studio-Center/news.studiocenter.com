Studio Center is pleased to announce the promotion of Katie Boyle to the company’s newly created position of Vice President of Audio Services.

Boyle, a graduate from the Honors Tutorial College at Ohio University with a degree in Audio Engineering, has been working as a Senior Audio Engineer at Studio Center since 2009.

Prior to coming to Studio Center, Boyle worked at XM Satellite Radio in Washington, D.C. as one of the first three engineers in the prestigious Performance Theatre.
